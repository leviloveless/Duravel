## FORMATS
- **DEKA FIT:** 10 zones, each preceded by 500 m run → **5,000 m total run**. ~30 min elite → 40–50 min AG. FIT uses throw(Z4)+yoke(Z8). WR M 28:28 (Schadegg 2022), W 31:51 (Weeks 2024). Slowest zone = sled push/pull. Energy: high-end aerobic / threshold intervals, but shorter/faster runs than HYROX → more anaerobic/repeat-effort. Limiters: running economy under fatigue, sled leg-drive, grip, lactate tolerance.
- **DEKA STRONG:** same 10 zones, **NO running, no cardio transitions**, back-to-back. WR M 10:33 (Schadegg 2023), W 11:55 (Wopat 2023). Bands (M): beginner ~29:00, intermediate ~19:20, advanced ~14:35, elite ~11:27. Energy: anaerobic-glycolytic / strength-endurance dominant (~10–20 min grip-and-grind). Limiters: row+ski back-to-back, sled, dead-ball shoulder-overs, grip. Running secondary.
- **DEKA MILE:** same 10 zones, each preceded by 160 m run → **1,600 m (1 mile) total**. WR M 16:04 (Kilgore 2024), W 18:43 (Fedorova 2025). Strong intermediate ~25–30 min. Energy: VO2max/anaerobic-capacity/repeat-sprint (160 m run hard). Between STRONG and FIT.
- **DEKA ULTRA (FIT ULTRA):** **5× consecutive DekaFit non-stop = 50 zones + 25 km run**. Cut-offs: AG 9 h; Elite 3:45 M / 4:15 W. Same FIT loads ×5. Energy: aerobic/muscular endurance (multi-hour). Challenge: fueling, repeated eccentric loading (lunges/box/sled ×5), grip/soft-tissue durability, pacing. Record leaderboard not yet published.
- **DEKA ATLAS:** DISTINCT heavier 10-zone set, **no running, 30-min cap**, Rx + Foundation (scaled) divisions.

## vs HYROX
HYROX = 8×1 km + 8 heavier/longer stations, ~60–90 min, ~50% running. DEKA FIT = shorter/faster 500 m runs + lighter/shorter stations → more interval/anaerobic; running ~60–65% of race time. MILE = pure repeat-sprint. STRONG/ATLAS = no running → CrossFit-chipper / grip-and-grind; ATLAS needs heaviest absolute strength. ULTRA = more endurance-heavy than HYROX (ultra-hybrid: fueling, durability, pacing).

## WEAKNESS DIAGNOSTICS (benchmark TTs for needs analysis)
Isolated zone time-trials + classics: 500 m row + 500 m ski back-to-back; 100 m sled push/pull at Rx (slowest zone → leg-drive); RAM burpee 20-for-time; dead-ball shoulder/wall-over 20-for-time; farmers 100 m @ 60/40 unbroken (grip); 500 m repeat-run economy (6–10×500 off short rest) for FIT; 160 m repeats for MILE; for ATLAS: 95 lb thruster capacity, unbroken DB S2OH endurance, 100 lb/hand carry.

## Published training
Mostly 12-week 3-phase (Foundation→Build→Race-specific w/ full 10-zone sims + taper). DEKA training vs HYROX = more intensity/explosiveness, fast interval repeats, circuit work, transition efficiency (fast on/off ergs, quick zone entries).
